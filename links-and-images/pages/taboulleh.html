ingredients

  • 3 cups finely chopped parsley
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • ¼ cup fine bulgur
  • 1 scallion
  • 4 tbsp freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 6 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ tsp sea salt

directions

  1. Combine parsley, scallions, tomatoes and bulgur in a medium bowl.
  2. Add olive oil, lemon juice, salt, (and other spices if using) mix well, taste and adjust seasoning to your taste.
  3. Serve immediately or refrigerate if serving later.
